Ad Billboard

Description

DIMENSIONS: 468 pixels wide by 60 pixels tall.

Add a single image to your header and select the destination page where users are taken when it is clicked. This works great for advertisements, but it is also useful to drive traffic to other parts of your own website like maybe a signup page for a newsletter.

NOTE If your image is not sized correctly before you upload, visual distortion will occur.

Settings

Upload

Click the Upload area then drag and drop your image onto the target as instructed.

Link URL

This is the destination for visitors who click the advertisement image. It can by an on-site or off-site link.
