Activity Tabs

Description

The Activity Tabs widget was designed to keep visitors on your site by creating a list of popular, active, or topical posts. Visitors often enter a site via a link to a single post, and not through the home page. In cases like this, it is considered good practice to provide a strategic list of additional posts to entice readers to stick around and read more articles.

Settings

Recent Posts

Show up to 10 of the latest published posts.

Most Commented Posts

Show up to 10 posts ordered by sequently by most comments to least comments.

Most Recent Comments

Show up to 10 comments order sequently by the most recent to the least recent.

Most Used Tags

Show up to 40 of the most used tags, in blocks of 10.

NOTE: If a tab is configured to show ’0′ items, the entire tab will be hidden from view for site visitors.
