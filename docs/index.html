Publishers. Lean’s design lets your content be the star of the show.

The theme is intentionally clean and simple so that it does not distract visitors from the most important thing of all, your content. It’s strategically organized and positioned each section so that visitors can immediately know who you are and what you want to communicate. There’s also an ad space if you want to monetize your content, all the typical social icons for visitors to find you on popular social sites. Perhaps the most important thing of all is that Lean Theme is loaded with SEO features that attract the attention of search engines like Google.

Designers, Developers. Lean’s design is practically a wireframe built on Bootstrap.

The design’s minimalist beginnings provide an empty canvas with which to start your designing endeavors. The style rocks because it won’t adversely influence your design before the creative process begins.

Lean is built on Bootstrap 3 and it’s child theme ready. Tired of installing a laundry-list of plugins for every project? Who isn’t? That’s why it’s baked them into Lean. Advertisements? Done. SEO? Done. Author biography box? Done. Configurable social icons? Yep. Native threaded comments with Gravatar support? Done and done.

When you choose Lean as the foundation of your next web project, you’re already ahead of the game. You no longer need to spend hours trying to get everyone else’s plugins to play nice with each other, ours are harmonious from the word go.
